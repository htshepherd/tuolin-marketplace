# LinkedIn Marketing Plan Review Checklist

## Market Fit

- The plan targets Europe and North America.
- The audience is B2B: industrial buyers, distributors, OEM buyers, maintenance teams, or thermal-management buyers.
- The content rhythm supports awareness, education, sourcing confidence, and inquiry conversion.

## Naming

- Use `特种玻璃纤维带` in Chinese-facing planning.
- Use `Specialty Glass Fiber Tape` in English-facing output guidance.
- Do not use quartz fiber as the public product name.

## Core Benefits

The plan must repeatedly support:

- `1000°C working temperature`
- `itch-free handling`
- `no smoke`

Use `no unpleasant odor` only as a secondary message when relevant.

## Claim Safety

Flag or remove unsupported public claims, including:

- eco-friendly
- carcinogen-free
- horsepower gain
- fixed noise reduction percentage
- fixed engine-bay temperature reduction
- A1 fire classification unless the report image/source is attached and reviewed
- asbestos-free unless the report image/source is attached and reviewed
- SiO2 content in public LinkedIn posts

## Workflow Compliance

- The plan must stop before the 30-day Chinese draft.
- The plan must not generate publishing images.
- The plan must state that LinkedIn posting is manual, not automated.
