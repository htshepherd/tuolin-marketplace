# Make evidence-first creative discovery the video workflow's primary capability

Tuolin video creation treats the pre-plan interview as the primary quality-determining capability. The workflow no longer uses a fixed six-question script, fixed creative-direction menu, `按推荐` shortcut, fixed shot count, or fixed five-second role sequence.

The user-facing skill owns contextual reasoning: it loads the formal product card and official images first, resolves one dependent business decision at a time, gives one recommendation with a concrete reason, challenges choices that offer spectacle without audience value, and accepts ordinary confirmation only for the visible current decision. The runtime persists decisions, evidence, provenance, and confirmation state and blocks incomplete or out-of-order transitions.

Every YouTube Shorts run performs a public trend scan after the audience problem is understood and before the final viewer-interest direction is confirmed. Evidence follows a relevance ladder from comparable industrial content through adjacent engineering/manufacturing content to safely transferable broader mechanisms. Sources, dates, mechanisms, relevance, exclusions, and degraded research states remain auditable. First-party channel analytics and TikTok are outside this release.

Before the final direction is confirmed, Codex opens and assesses all candidate official images. Pixels determine visual feasibility but never product truth. An unsupported direction must be revised, shortened, or limited to explicitly approved evidence-safe AI simulation.

The storyboard and standard SRT are created and confirmed together as the sole shot-level creative contract. A shot may contain an ordered sequence of images when continuity is defensible. Prompt artifacts are generated only after confirmation and remain an internal provider translation. Provider inability to execute a confirmed multi-image sequence requires a visible storyboard revision and renewed confirmation.

The operator monitors Dreamina generation in the web interface. After the operator reports all tasks complete, the system performs one download using real task IDs and validates all files before assembly. User-facing status polling, silent retries, single-shot regeneration, and result improvement are not implemented. Rejected results stop the run and require a new video task.
