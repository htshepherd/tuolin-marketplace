# Replace fixed video directions with an internalized creation interview

Tuolin removes the fixed 16-direction taxonomy, primary/supporting direction selection, and ranked direction recommendations. `tuolin-video-workflow` remains the only user-facing video entrypoint and internalizes a one-question-at-a-time interview that produces a confirmed brief directly from business-language answers. This preserves one owner for each isolated video run, avoids asking non-professional users to choose inaccurate creative categories, and feeds the existing plan, storyboard, Dreamina, and assembly stages without introducing a second Agent boundary or external `grill-me` dependency.

Before a plan is shown, the same workflow performs an internal pixel-level review of every shortlisted image and records usable, distinct material choices. Material capacity is a confirmation gate: insufficient distinct images require either a shorter duration or explicit deliberate-repetition approval. Natural-language semantic revisions are applied through controlled write paths that allow creative fields while protecting product identity, knowledge boundaries, format, source assets, and confirmation gates. Rejected generated results stop the run and are recorded; post-generation improvement remains outside this release.
