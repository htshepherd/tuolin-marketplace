# Keep video creation behind one consumer workflow

The quartz-fiber-tape video agent is an application-layer consumer that reads the product card and explicitly related content assets only through the `video_creation` Agent context. `tuolin-video-workflow` is the sole user-facing entrypoint for creative discovery, planning, storyboard plus SRT confirmation, internal Dreamina prompts, task planning, manual submission handoff, one-shot result download, and assembly. Post-generation improvement, narration audio, music, subtitle burn-in, publishing, and final editing are intentionally outside this Agent; ordinary users should not need to scan raw materials, invoke internal scripts, or depend on a specific media provider.

Update: the team considered adding open-source TTS voiceover generation to solve silent previews, but rejected it for the current upgrade because voice quality and review risk are better handled manually in Jianying/CapCut. The confirmed `storyboard.srt` is the sole future verbatim narration transcript. The Agent does not create a second voiceover document, synthesize audio, burn subtitles, or own final audio decisions.
