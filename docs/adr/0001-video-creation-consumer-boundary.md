# Keep video creation behind one consumer workflow

The quartz-fiber-tape video agent is an application-layer consumer that reads the product card and explicitly related content assets only through the `video_creation` Agent context. `tuolin-video-workflow` is the sole user-facing entrypoint for interview, planning, storyboard, Dreamina prompts, Dreamina task planning, manual submission handoff, result querying, shot review, and assembly. Post-generation improvement, narration audio, music, subtitles, and final editing are intentionally outside this Agent; ordinary users should not need to scan raw materials, invoke internal scripts, or depend on a specific media provider.

Update: the team considered adding open-source TTS voiceover generation to solve silent previews, but rejected it for the current upgrade because voice quality and review risk are better handled manually in Jianying/CapCut. The Agent may still write voiceover copy and editing handoff notes, but it does not synthesize voiceover audio or own final audio decisions.
